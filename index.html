Classic Chocolate Chip Cookies

By Aaron Kaipf, published on April 08, 2024

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 175 degrees C.
  2. Combine flour and baking soda in a bowl.
  3. In another bowl, cream together butter and sugars.
  4. Beat in the egg and vanilla extract.
  5. Gradually blend in the flour mixture.
  6. Stir in the chocolate chips.
  7. Drop by tablespoon onto ungreased cookie sheets.
  8. Bake 8 to 10 minutes or until lightly browned.
