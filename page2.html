Description: Total Recall is the unbelievably true story of Arnold Schwarzenegger's life.
Born in the small city of Thal, Austria, in 1947, he moved to the United States in 1968.
Within ten years, he was a millionaire businessman. After twenty years, he was the world's biggest movie star.
In 2003, he was Governor of California and a household name around the world.
